  1. Your fighter must win to earn additional points
  2. Additional points include getting either the 'round' or 'how' correct
  3. If both are correct a bonus will ensue due to you getting the pick perfect

A combination of getting the categories 'Winner' + 'Round' + 'How' correct will determine your score

Prelim

Winner = +2

Round = +1

How = +1

If all three are correct a 'Perfect' +5 bonus will be awarded for a total of 9 points

Main

Winner = +3

Round = +1

How = +1

If all three are correct a 'Perfect' +5 bonus will be awarded for a total of 10 points
